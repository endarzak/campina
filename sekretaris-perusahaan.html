Sagita Melati

Sekretaris Perusahaan

Sagita Melati serves as Corporate Secretary since 25 March 2019 based on the decision letter of BoD dated 25 March 2019. Her appointment was reported to the Indonesian Financial Services Authority (Otoritas Jasa Keuangan) and published through the Campina website and the Indonesia Stock Exchange on 27 March 2019.

Sagita is based in Surabaya, she holds Bachelor of Law from Hang Tuah University, Magister of Management from Airlangga University. Before being appointed as the Corporate Secretary, she was the Associate of Corporate Secretary PT. Campina Ice Cream Industry, Tbk (2018-2019) and Associate of Corporate Secretary of PT Steel Pipe Industry of Indonesia Tbk (2017-2018).
